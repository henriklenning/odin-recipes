Amaretto Apple Sour

Ingredients

  • 3 cl Old Tom Gin, 2 cl Amaretto, 3 cl lemon juice, 2 cl syrup and 2 cl eggwhites.

    • 1. Pour all the ingredients into a shaker
      2. Shake without ice to get a nice foam from the eggwhites
      3. Add ice to shake the drink cold
      4. Rinse into a glass and garnish with a dried appleslice
